About Us

‘Tyndis Travel’ nurtured the concept
of responsible tourism.

Almost all well-known travel agencies create mass packages for tourists. Backed with a decade of experience working for one such company, Naveen got together with his team to lovingly nurture the concept of responsible tourism through their baby ‘Tyndis Travel’. Initiated in 2018,The dual purpose of this initiative is to impart unique travel experiences to tourists visiting South India and uplift the local community. 

Our journey so far has truly been an incredible one! As a team, we are obsessed with the idea of empowering travelers by unfolding the stories of the places we take them to. We suggest boarding facilities depending on the traveler’s interest. From a backpacker’s needs to a honeymooner’s demands, from a cultural enthusiast to a culinary buff, we tailor-make thoughtfully crafted itineraries for tourists .

Besides entertaining tourists, we cater to the exclusive needs of corporates. We handpick mood-elevating venues for workations , Curate corporate travel tours for senior management , Conduct leisure team picnics and Execute annual meets, conferences, and offsite corporate events with the utmost professionalism.Tyndis Travel has a passionate team that strives together to be Earth's best travel firm. We craft vacations and events that guarantee personalized memories for a lifetime. Probably, this is the reason why we have customers come back to us year after year or recommend us to their friends and family .
